# THE PHONEBOOK
**The phonebook** is a user based interphase based on the oldschool yellow pages, which helped link communities and are very underappreciated as the were refined for their time.
## PROJECT BRIEF
The phonebook is meant ti improve the ease in which people search for services , including emergency services, which is a gap in most developed countries as there are no established platforms on which they can do so. we are here to change that. 
You need a plumber,electrician...or even just a place to enjoy lunch with the kids, we got you.
## FUNDAMENTALS 
The project will be set up with live location, a search engine and a platform whereby businesses can register in order to list themselves or their job openings.
As as now, what we have achieved is with *HTML AND CSS*
## BUSINESS RATIONAL
Businesses will pay a monthly or yearly fee to enlist, taking the burden from the users. Users tend to prefer free items instead of premium items, hence they may more likely to use it if it were free to them.
